Honey BBQ Chicken Wings

Play the video

Recipe Details


1.Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2.Mix flour, chili powder, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder in a bowl.
3.Coat wings evenly in the flour mixture, shaking off excess.
4.Place wings on parchment paper–lined baking sheet in a single layer.
5.Bake for 45 minutes, flipping halfway, until crispy and golden.
6.Preheat oven to 500°F (250°C).
7.Combine BBQ sauce and honey in a bowl.
8.Toss cooked wings in the sauce to coat evenly.
9.Place coated wings back on the baking sheet in a single layer.
10.Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until sauce is bubbly and caramelized.
11.Cool and serve. Enjoy!
